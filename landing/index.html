About Chesso

Chesso is my personal project that I'm building to help myself memorize chess openings more effectively.

As a chess player, I've always struggled with remembering complex opening lines and understanding the strategic ideas behind them. Existing tools were either too complex or too basic for practical training.

That's why I built Chesso - an intuitive, open-source platform that helps me build, manage, and train with my personal chess opening repertoire.
